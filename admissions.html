How to Apply Documents

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Age 3–4 years as on April 1
  • UKG: Age 4–5 years as on April 1
  • Classes I–XII: Passed previous class from a recognised school

Admission Window

  • Nursery to UKG: Starts in January
  • Classes I–XII: Starts in March
  • Steps: Registration → Interaction/Assessment (if applicable) → Principal Interaction → Final List

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Any Proof of Age
  • Aadhaar Card (student)
  • Parents’ Aadhaar Card
  • Previous School Transfer/Leaving Certificate
  • e‑Punjab Leaving Certificate
  • Previous Class Result Card
  • 4 Passport‑size Photographs (student)

Please carry originals for verification and submit clear photocopies.
