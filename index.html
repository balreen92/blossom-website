About Us

Established in 2008 by our Chairman Mr. Satnam Singh Sandhu, Blossom Public Senior Secondary School serves families across Village Chakar (Tehsil Jagraon, Ludhiana) with a warm, student-centred approach to education. We offer classes from Nursery to XII and combine strong academics with values, sports, arts and life skills. Our qualified faculty, modern science and computer labs, well-stocked library and safe, supervised campus ensure every child learns with confidence. At Blossom, we believe in “High… Higher… Still Higher” — nurturing curious minds, kind hearts and responsible citizens.
